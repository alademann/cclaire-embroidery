Frequently Asked Questions

What types of payments do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and Discover.  We also accept checks and money orders.  However, when paying by check or money order we will not begin work on your order until payment is received and cleared.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes, your order can be changed or cancelled prior to us beginning work on it.  Each order is custom made; therefore, once work has begun it cannot be changed or cancelled.  If you need to cancel an order or modify it please contact us the same day that the order is placed.

What is your return policy?

We work hard to provide our customers with quality products.  We inspect each item prior to beginning embroidery work and after our work is complete to ensure that each item meets our standards.  Upon receipt of your order please inspect your purchase.  We do not accept returns on personalized items unless an error was made on the part of C. Claire Embroidery, LLC or manufacturers defect.  If you find that your order was made incorrectly, an error on our part, please notify us immediately.  All errors must be reported within 7 days.  If the error was on the part of the customer we cannot accept a return.  Therefore, please verify that the information that you provide us at the time of your order is accurate.

Do you share information about suppliers?

No, we do not share information about our suppliers to customers or businesses.
